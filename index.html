Why did I become a developer?

As a developer, I can freely create software, web sites from the knowledge I have learned. Being in Software Development isn’t quite the same as being a Graphic Designer or Fine Artist, but that doesn’t mean it’s not creative in its own way. I love solving problems, knowing that the software I make helps make someones life, job, or hobby easier/faster/better. I'm happy for that.

Alongside learning about technology, developers will often have the opportunity to develop their knowledge about the business and sector they are working in, as these are also ever-changing. This constant change is an amazing learning opportunity, keeping my mind fresh and the job interesting.
